About Us

Welcome to our website! Established in 1936, we take pride in being the oldest Purina dealer serving the East. With a rich history spanning nearly a century, our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains unwavering.
In 1997, we underwent a change in ownership, rejuvenating our vision while staying true to our roots. This transition ushered in a new era of innovation and service excellence, allowing us to evolve alongside the ever-changing needs of our valued customers.
Nestled within our establishment is a charming green house adorned with lush plants, serving as both a decorative oasis and a hub for agriculture enthusiasts. Here, customers can explore a diverse selection of flora while seeking expert advice from our knowledgeable staff.
Beyond our botanical offerings, we boast a comprehensive range of products and services tailored to meet the needs of our diverse clientele. Whether you're in search of premium mulch to enhance your garden or seeking essential hardware supplies for your latest project, our well-stocked shelves have you covered.
Furthermore, as a trusted feed store, we provide top-quality feed and nutritional supplements for a variety of animals, ensuring their health and vitality. Our dedication to animal welfare extends beyond mere transactions; it's a testament to our commitment to fostering thriving communities.
At our core, we're more than just a store; we're a cornerstone of the community, fostering connections and providing solutions that enrich lives. As we continue to grow and adapt, our passion for serving you remains steadfast, driving us to exceed expectations and set the standard for excellence in everything we do. Thank you for choosing us as your trusted partner on your journey towards a greener, more vibrant future.
